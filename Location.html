We are located at

Kape G's coffee cart is located at 1123 Antipolo Street, Brgy Valenzuela, Makati City. Set in the lively outdoors, our cart provides a charming and relaxed ambiance where you can savor our freshly brewed coffee and delightful treats. Whether you're stopping by for a quick pick-me-up, enjoying a leisurely chat with friends, or simply appreciating the open-air vibe, Kape G's offers a delightful experience for coffee enthusiasts in the heart of the city.
