About us:

Welcome to Kape G! We are a small, cozy coffee stall dedicated to bringing you the finest selection of hot and cold coffee beverages. Our passion for great coffee drives us to source only the highest quality beans and create delicious, freshly brewed drinks that will make your day a little brighter.



At Kape G, we believe in crafting coffee with care and precision, ensuring every cup is made to perfection. Our skilled baristas are here to serve you with a smile and create a welcoming atmosphere that feels like home. Whether you're looking for a classic hot coffee to warm your soul or a refreshing cold brew to cool down, we have something for everyone.





Hours of Operation:



We are open from 12 PM to 5 PM, perfect for an afternoon pick-me-up or a relaxed coffee break.



Join us at Kape G for a delightful coffee experience. Whether you're on the go or looking to relax, our friendly team is ready to serve you a cup of happiness. Thank you for choosing Kape G! We can't wait to share our passion for coffee with you.
